Buying & Orders

Everything you need to know about purchasing products from our vendors.

How do I place an order?

expand_more
Browsing Voltamarket is easy. Simply search for the product you need, click "Add to Cart," and proceed to checkout. You will need to create an account or log in to finalize your purchase. We deliver directly to your doorstep in major towns like Ho and Kpando.

Can I pay via Mobile Money?

expand_more
Yes, absolutely! We accept all major mobile money networks including MTN Momo, Vodafone Cash, and AirtelTigo Money. Select 'Mobile Money' at checkout, enter your number, and approve the prompt on your phone.

How do I track my delivery?

expand_more
Once your order is dispatched, you will receive an SMS and email notification. You can also go to "My Orders" in your account dashboard to see real-time updates as your package moves through the Volta Region. Alternatively, use our Order Tracking page.

What if an item is out of stock?

expand_more
If an item is out of stock, you can choose to be notified when it becomes available again. In rare cases where an order is placed for an out-of-stock item, we will process an immediate refund to your mobile money wallet.

Do you deliver to Oti Region?

expand_more
Yes, Voltamarket covers both Volta and Oti regions. Delivery times may vary slightly for deeper Oti locations like Dambai or Nkwanta depending on road conditions and vendor location.
