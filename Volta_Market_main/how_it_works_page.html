Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept? expand_more
We accept Mobile Money (MTN Momo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money) and all major VISA and Mastercard debit/credit cards.
How long does delivery take? expand_more
Deliveries within major towns in Volta and Oti typically take 24-48 hours. Remote areas may take up to 3 business days.
Can I return an item if I don't like it? expand_more
Yes, you have a 7-day return window for most items, provided they are unused and in their original packaging. Please check our Return Policy for exclusions.
