check_circle Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions. Please ensure you check these before initiating a request.

How to Return an Item

assignment_return

1. Request Return

Log in to your account, go to "My Orders", and select "Return Item".

local_shipping

2. Ship Item

Pack the item securely. Drop it off at our nearest partner hub in Ho or Dambai.

payments

3. Receive Refund

Once inspected, your refund will be processed to your original payment method.

info

Shipping Costs

If the return is due to our error (wrong item, damaged), we cover shipping. Otherwise, return shipping fees are deducted from your refund.

Non-Returnable Items

warning

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Perishable goods such as food, flowers, or newspapers.
  • Intimate or sanitary goods (e.g., underwear, swimwear, personal care items).
  • Hazardous materials, or flammable liquids or gases.
  • Gift cards and downloadable software products.
  • Items returned more than 14 days after delivery.

Refund Timelines

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed within the following timelines:

Payment Method Estimated Time
Mobile Money (Momo) 24 - 48 Hours
Bank Card / Credit Card 5 - 10 Business Days
Volta Wallet Credit Instant
