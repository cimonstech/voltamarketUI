About this Service

Experience the rich heritage of the Volta Region with our authentic Kente weaving services. Whether you need a custom-designed cloth for a special occasion (weddings, festivals, naming ceremonies) or want to learn the ancient art of weaving yourself, Master Weaver Kwame Osei brings 20+ years of expertise directly to you.

Based in Agotime-Kpetoe, the Kente capital, we source only the finest threads and use traditional looms to ensure every piece tells a story.

What we offer

  • check_circle Custom Kente design and weaving for individuals and groups
  • check_circle Traditional loom setup and maintenance services
  • check_circle Beginner to advanced weaving workshops (weekend & intensive)
  • check_circle Restoration of vintage Kente cloths
