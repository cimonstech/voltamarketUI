Seller Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: October 24, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Voltamarket. These terms govern your use of our platform to sell goods and services in Ghana. By registering as a seller, you agree to comply with all applicable laws and regulations in Ghana, including but not limited to the Electronic Transactions Act. Please read these terms carefully before proceeding.

Voltamarket acts as a marketplace connecting sellers with buyers. We are not a party to the contracts for sale between third-party sellers and buyers.

2. Account Registration

To sell on Voltamarket, you must provide accurate, current, and complete information during the registration process. This includes your business name, contact information, and valid payment details.

info
Verification Required Sellers must verify their identity using a Ghana Card or valid Passport within 7 days of registration.

3. Selling & Listing Policies

All product listings must be clear, accurate, and not misleading. You agree to list products in the appropriate category and include all relevant details such as condition, size, and specifications. Images must accurately represent the product being sold.

  • Duplicate listings for the same item are prohibited.
  • Watermarked images containing contact information are not allowed.
  • Prices must be listed in Ghana Cedis (GHS) and include all applicable taxes.

4. Fees & Payouts

Voltamarket charges a commission fee on each successful sale. The commission rate varies by category, ranging from 3% to 12%.

Category Commission Fee Closing Fee
Electronics & Gadgets 5% GHS 2.00
Fashion & Apparel 10% GHS 1.50
Home & Living 8% GHS 3.00
